Welcome to my website!

The purpose of this page is to explore FBI's Most Wanted Criminals. The goal is for people to have easier access to this information, and potentially be able to provide information to the FBI about these individuals. A lot of them are dangerous, so it is important to get their information out there so they don't cause more harm.

On this site you can find:

  • Information about the API itself
  • A roulette of the First 10 on the Wanted list
  • A map of where the First 10 committed their crimes
  • A chart that shows the gender distribution for the crimes
  • A form you can submit tips about any of the criminals
